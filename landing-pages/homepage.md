## FAQ

**Q: How does the color analysis work?**
A: You upload a selfie and our AI analyzes your skin undertone, contrast level, hair color, and eye color to determine your color season using the professional 12-season system. We then validate with a personalized quiz. The result tells you exactly which colors flatter you and which to avoid.

**Q: Which brands do you shop from?**
A: Currently Zara, Uniqlo, and Quince. We chose these for their range of styles, consistent quality at accessible prices, and frequent new arrivals.

**Q: How accurate is the AI scoring?**
A: Each product is scored across five dimensions: color match, silhouette fit for your body shape, fabric quality, climate suitability, and alignment with your style personality. Products must score 60+ to appear in your feed. Most users find the recommendations highly accurate after completing the style quiz.

**Q: What if I do not like the recommendations?**
A: You can skip any product and swap pieces within outfits. The more you interact, the better the curation becomes. You are always in control of what you save and buy.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Your photos are analyzed once to build your profile and are never stored on our servers. Your style profile is saved securely so your recommendations stay personalized.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Creating your style profile and getting personalized recommendations is free.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: About five minutes. Upload two photos, answer a short quiz, and your curated wardrobe is ready.

3. Is it legit? -- PARTIAL. Stats bar helps. Needs real testimonials.

7. How do I act? -- YES. Clear CTA throughout.

### Copy Quality Gate (Harry Dry's 3 Rules)
- Hero header: "Your personal stylist that shops while you work."
  - Visualize? YES -- you can picture someone shopping on your behalf.
  - Falsify? YES -- it either shops while you work or it does not.
  - Nobody else can say it? YES -- the "while you work" framing is specific to an AI agent.

### Conversion Formula Audit
- Desire: STRONG. Identity-based hook + clear time savings + professional outcome.
- Confidence: MEDIUM. Needs real testimonials and social proof. Stats help.
- Effort: LOW (good). 5-minute setup, free, clear CTA.
- Confusion: LOW (good). Three-step process is clear. One CTA throughout.
